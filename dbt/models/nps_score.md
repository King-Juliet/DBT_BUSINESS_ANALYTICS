NPS, Net Promoter Score, is used to measure a customer's loyalty by understanding the likelihood of the customer recommending your business and its services.

-----------------------------
| NPS       |  Description  |
|---------------------------|
| 0 - 6     |  Detractors   |
| 7 - 8     |  Passives     |
| 9 - 10    |  Promoters    |
-----------------------------
